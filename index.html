Healthy Recipes

  • Day 1: Quick Overnight Oats

    Combine oats, almond milk, banana, honey, and salt. Refrigerate overnight.

  • Day 2: One-Pot Pasta

    Cook pasta with tomatoes, spinach, olive oil, garlic, salt, and pepper.

  • Day 3: Avocado Toast

    Toast bread, top with mashed avocado, and add toppings like eggs or tomatoes.

  • Day 4: Slow Cooker Chicken and Rice

    Cook chicken, rice, veggies, and broth in a slow cooker for 6-8 hours.

  • Day 5: Quesadilla

    Fill tortilla with cheese, chicken, and veggies. Cook until crispy.

  • Day 6: Smoothie Bowl

    Blend frozen berries, banana, almond milk, and top with granola.

  • Day 7: Breakfast Burrito

    Fill tortilla with scrambled eggs, sausage, cheese, and beans. Cook until crispy.
