Your guide to a healthier lifestyle with a busy schedule!

1. Exercise

Day 1: Monday (Upper Body)

  • Push-ups: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Arm circles: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Tricep dips (using a chair): 3 sets of 10 reps

Day 2: Tuesday (Lower Body)

  • Squats: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Calf raises: 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Glute bridges: 3 sets of 10 reps

2. Healthy Recipes

Day 1: Quick Overnight Oats

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1/2 banana, sliced
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions: Combine all ingredients in a jar. Refrigerate overnight and enjoy!

Day 2: One-Pot Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup whole wheat pasta
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 1 cup frozen spinach

Instructions: Cook all ingredients together and serve.
