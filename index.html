2. Healthy Recipes

Day 1: Quick Overnight Oats

  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1/2 banana, sliced
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions: Combine all ingredients in a jar or container. Refrigerate overnight and enjoy in the morning.

Day 2: One-Pot Pasta

  • 1 cup whole wheat pasta
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 1 cup frozen spinach
  • 1/4 cup olive oil

Instructions: Cook pasta, tomatoes, and spinach in a large pot. Add olive oil and garlic.

Day 3: Avocado Toast

  • 2 slices whole grain bread
  • 1 ripe avocado,mashed
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 egg sliced
  • cherry tomatoes

instructions:Toast bread, mash avocado, and top with eggs or tomatoes.

Day 4:slow cooker chicken and rice

  • 1 lb boneless,skinless chicken breast
  • 1 cup uncooked whiterice
  • 2 cups mixed frozen vegetable
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • salt and pepper

Instructions: combine all ingredients in a small cooker.cook on slow for 6 to 8 hours.

Day 5:Quesadilla

  • 1 whole wheat tortilla
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese
  • 1/2 cup cooked chicken breast
  • 1/4 cup chopped bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup chopped onions
  • salsa and sour cream

Instructions: fill tortilla with cheese,chicken,bell peppers and onions.cook in a pan until crispy and melted.

Day 6:smoothie bowl

  • 1 cup frozen mixed berries
  • 1/2 banana slice
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds
  • granola,nuts and seeds

Instructions: blend all ingredients in blender.top with granola,nuts and seeds

Day 7:Burrito

  • 1 whole wheat tortilla
  • 2 scrambled eggs
  • 1/4 cooked sausages
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheese
  • 1/4 cup cannrd black beans
  • salsa and sour cream

Instructions: fill tortilla with scrambled eggs,sausage,cheese and black beans.cook in a pan until crispy and melted
