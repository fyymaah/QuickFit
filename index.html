Healthy Recipe

  • Day 1: Quick Overnight Oats
    • 1/2 cup rolled oats
    • 1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk
    • 1/2 banana, sliced
    • 1 tablespoon honey
    • Pinch of salt
    • Top with nuts or seeds (optional)
  • Instruction: Combine all ingredients in a jar or container. Refrigerate overnight and enjoy in the morning.
  • Day 2: One-Pot Pasta
    • 1 cup whole wheat pasta
    • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
    • 1 cup frozen spinach
    • 1/4 cup olive oil
    • 1 clove garlic, minced
    • Salt and pepper to taste
    • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)
  • Instruction: Cook pasta, tomatoes, and spinach in a large pot. Add olive oil, garlic, salt, and pepper. Top with Parmesan cheese, if desired.
